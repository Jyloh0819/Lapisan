Acar Awak

Cut the vegetables into thin slices. Soak the julienned cucumber and white radish with white vinegar for more crunchiness. Leave it aside for later.

Prepare the toppings by roasting the peanuts until it looks slightly burnt and blend the roasted peanuts into crumbs. Put them aside for later.

To prepare the paste, cut the dried chillies into pieces, slice the shallots, lemongrass and red ginger. Finally, blend all the chilli paste ingredients together until a paste is formed.

Fry the chilli paste with oil in medium to high heat in a pan until fragrant.

Follow by adding the assam paste into the pan and slowly add the julienned vegetables into the pan altogether. Stir them well and add in sugar according to taste.
